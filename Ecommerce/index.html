ABOUT US

What makes SPIZE unique is its emphasis on using a hand mill to ground spices along with procuring the best certified organic spices from all across India. The products are handmade, 100% Certified Organic, hand-ground hand-cleaned and hand-pounded. Manufacturing spices on hand mills allow spices to breathe and retain all their nutrients and aroma in it, making them fuller, fresher and pure.

They are sold through a few organic and modern trade channels in Chennai and also through Amazon, Flipkart and its own website (www.spize.in). SPIZE spices are exported abroad. This has helped the brand to build a strong reputation and presence in foreign countries.
